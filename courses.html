Courses

These courses are designed to introduce students to important concepts in mechanical engineering through lessons, worked examples, quizzes, real-world applications, and experiment demonstrations. Each course focuses on a specific topic and is intended to build both engineering knowledge and problem-solving skills. Whether you are learning these concepts for the first time, reviewing for a class, or exploring engineering as a future career, these courses provide a structured way to learn and practice. Most courses include:

  • Lessons – Learn the fundamental concepts and equations.
  • Worked Example Problems – See step-by-step solutions.
  • Quizzes – Test your understanding with randomized questions.
  • Real World Applications – Discover how engineers use these concepts.
  • Experiment Demonstrations – Watch concepts applied through hands-on experiments.
